Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

Laugh Circuit Media respects user privacy.

This app uses platform login and authorization only to connect social media accounts and publish short-form video content to connected accounts, including TikTok and YouTube.

The app may access basic account information and publishing permissions only when the user gives permission through the relevant platform's authorization process.

Access tokens may be used only for account authorization and content publishing through the connected account.

Laugh Circuit Media does not sell personal information.

Laugh Circuit Media does not share user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Access tokens and authorization data are used only for the purpose of managing and publishing content through connected social media accounts.

Users can revoke access at any time through their TikTok, YouTube, or Google account settings.

Laugh Circuit Media takes reasonable steps to keep authorization data secure. However, users should not share private access tokens, passwords, or account credentials with anyone.
